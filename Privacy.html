Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Poki ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our Discord bot.

Information We Collect

We collect the following information:

  • Discord User ID
  • Discord Username
  • Server IDs where the bot is used
  • Game progress data (Pokémon collection, virtual currency balances, etc.)
  • Command usage statistics
  • Vote history

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain the bot's functionality
  • Store your game progress and Pokémon collection
  • Track virtual currency transactions
  • Improve user experience
  • Prevent abuse and enforce our Terms of Service

Data Storage and Security

  • User data is stored in a secure database
  • We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information
  • We retain your data as long as you actively use the bot or until you request deletion

Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services for:

  • Bot hosting
  • Database management
  • Vote tracking (Top.gg, Discord Bot List)

These services have their own privacy policies governing how they handle your data.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out of certain data collection

To exercise these rights, contact us through our Support Server.

Data Deletion

To request deletion of your data, please contact us through our Support Server. We will process your request within 30 days.

Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any significant changes through our Support Server or Discord bot announcements.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our Support Server.
